Climatique is a game developed by Cypher Studios that focuses on developing knowledge about sustainable development. The goal of the game is to add points by catching the proper garbage into the proper basket. There will be 2 baskets. One is for recyclable materials and the other is for non-recyclable materials. The more points you gain, the more clean your environment becomes, the more misses you do, the dirtier it gets and the CO2 percent increases. If it increases beyond a certain threshold, your environment is doomed and you will have to start all over again.
